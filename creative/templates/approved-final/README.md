# One Life Approved Final Creative References

Corrected: 2026-06-12

This folder contains the canonical approved June and July 2026 cinematic posting packs for Mon/Wed/Thu promotional creative.

Use these files before rendering or revising One Life social, Telegram, WhatsApp, IG/FB, email, or GBP promotional creative.

## Canonical Folders

- `june-2026/`: final June 2026 approved team posting pack.
- `july-2026/`: final July 2026 approved cinematic posting pack.
- `reference/approved-three-template-screenshot.jpg`: quick visual reference for the three standing template families.

## Template Families

| Family | Approved label examples | Use for |
|---|---|---|
| Monday Hero | `MONDAY HERO`, `MONDAY OFFER` | Single product or primary offer lead |
| Vivid Wednesday | `VIVID WEDNESDAY`, `WEDNESDAY OFFER` | Vivid branded duo/product routine |
| Thursday Bundle | `THURSDAY BUNDLE`, `THURSDAY OFFER` | Multi-product bundle/stack offer |

## Do Not Use As Final Direction

The following folders are superseded renderer samples and are not the approved final visual direction:

- `creative/templates/existing/`
- `creative/templates/T02/sample/`
- `creative/templates/T03/sample/`
- `creative/templates/T04/sample/`

They may be useful for understanding old slot names, but they should not drive design quality, layout, or approval decisions.

## Production Rules

- Match the approved cinematic green/gold poster treatment.
- Use real product packshots and verified product data.
- Verify pricing, savings, date ranges, stock, and branch availability before publishing.
- Keep claims structure/function only and preserve the footer disclaimer.
- Do not generate fake product labels, fake people, or new brand directions unless the brief explicitly asks for a new approval sample.
