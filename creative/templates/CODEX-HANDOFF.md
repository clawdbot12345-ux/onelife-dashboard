## Required Workflow

1. Read `creative/templates/README.md`.
2. Read `creative/templates/existing/MANIFEST.md`.
3. For Mon/Wed/Thu work, inspect the relevant existing sample images before writing copy or rendering.
4. Extend the template family by swapping copy slots, packshots, prices, CTA variants, and date ranges.
5. Do not invent a new visual direction unless a brief explicitly asks for a new template and review sample.

## Quality Bar

- Use cinematic raster/product-poster treatment from the approved samples.
- Use real/public Shopify packshots; do not AI-redraw labels.
- Keep offer math exact and GP-safe when a brief includes prices or discounts.
- Keep claims structure/function only and include the product disclaimer where relevant.
- Avoid crude icon-card layouts, generic stock looks, fake people, and busy explanatory slides.
