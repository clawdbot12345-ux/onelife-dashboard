## 2. The six engines (priority order)

### Engine 1 — Checkout Rescue 🔴 week 1
319 abandons/mo at the payment step. Actions: install Payflex + PayJustNow
(stacks are the BNPL sweet spot), add Ozow, audit checkout for delivery-cost
surprise, re-add BNPL badges to cart trust strip once live.
**Owner:** 2 app installs. **Claude:** badge/UX updates + verify funnel weekly.
Expected: +R85–190k/mo.

### Engine 2 — Conversion Layer (trust)
Zero reviews on PDPs is the #1 trust hole. Judge.me post-purchase emails +
R25 credit for first 200 reviews. Codex product-image pilot (top 50 sellers).
**Owner:** approve incentive. **Codex:** images. **Claude:** review-request email + measurement.
Expected: session→cart 2.1%→4%+.

### Engine 3 — Retention Machine (18%→40% repeat)
Already built today: Klaviyo cadence (welcome/cart/browse/winback/back-in-stock)
+ list growth engine (Codex Part E) + single opt-in. Remaining: **Appstle
subscriptions** (first mover in SA premium health) and **WhatsApp broadcast
channel** (Precious's natural medium; SA's highest-attention channel).
Expected: email/WhatsApp revenue R17k→R60k+/mo; repeat compounding.

### Engine 4 — Store→Online Flywheel (the uncopyable advantage)
R2.15m/mo of store sales = tens of thousands of monthly till transactions,
almost none captured digitally. Actions: till-slip QR → R50 first-online-order
voucher; counter email capture with R5/email staff incentive; "order online,
collect today" in-store signage; Smile loyalty unified across channels.
2% capture = 1,000+ zero-CAC warm customers/mo.
**Owner:** print + brief store staff. **Claude:** voucher codes, landing page, tracking.

### Engine 5 — Traffic (only after the bucket holds water)
SEO machine is live and compounding (17 landers, 125 selling articles, schema).
Next: Google Merchant Center free listings (ready); paid restart (R60–100k/mo,
Meta-led) once conversion >2% so ROAS ~3× is realistic; GLP-1 telehealth
partnership outreach (protocol + guide = the pitch deck, nobody in SA doing it);
practitioner B2B channel activation (page is live, needs outreach).

### Engine 6 — Brand Moat (leading health brand in SA)
1. **The Apothecary story** — family vs corporate, 30 years, consultants who
   tell you NOT to buy. PR push: SA business/lifestyle media love this story.
2. **Vivid house brand** — margin + exclusivity; expand range + wholesale later.
3. **Precious video engine** — Ask Precious / shelf tours / myth-busting;
   the trust flywheel that feeds every other engine.
4. **Edenvale local playbook** — GBP optimization, radius Meta ads, in-store
   events, local WhatsApp community. It's an awareness problem, not a store problem.
