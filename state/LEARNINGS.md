# LEARNINGS — distilled, permanent insights (what works for OUR customers)

Seeded 2026-06-12 from prior session evidence in this repo. Every entry cites its source.

1. **Image-heavy emails cut clicks.** One hero image max, text-first, voice-of-Precious narrative. (HubSpot 500M-email data + AG1/Ritual teardowns; decided 2026-06-11, email-cadence-system-2026.md)
2. **Full-list sends cost 1–2% unsubs per send.** Campaigns target Engaged 90d only. (Klaviyo audit 2026-06-10)
3. **Welcome flow email 1 performs at elite level** (R97/recipient vs $2.35 industry avg). The voice works — keep it. (email-cadence-system-2026.md)
4. **~13k SEO sessions/mo land on commodity PDPs and convert at zero** because R130 shipping on a R173 product is a 75% surcharge. Shipping shock, not traffic, is the leak. (growth-plan-2026-06.md)
5. **83% of traffic is mobile and there's no express checkout** — checkout completion 40% vs 65% healthy. Biggest single conversion lever; owner action pending. (growth-plan-2026-06.md)
6. **Gauteng = ~60% of online revenue.** "Order by 12pm, collect at Centurion today, talk to Precious" is the moat no national player can copy. (growth-plan-2026-06.md)
7. **Returning-customer rate collapsed 33.8%→15.7% over TTM** (verified live 2026-06-12). Retention, not acquisition, is the structural problem online.
8. **Paid (R19.5k/mo) produces ~zero attributable revenue** while free channels (Google free listings 33%, organic 26%, direct 25%) carry the store. Attribution must be fixed before any paid scaling. (weekly business report 2026-06-12)
9. **SAHPRA claim rules are precise and non-negotiable**: structure/function verbs only (contributes/assists/helps/aids/supports/maintains), no disease claims, mandatory unregistered-medicine disclaimer. (research/SA_MARKET.md, sourced)
10. **The social gap is specifically Instagram (1.4K), not social overall** — Facebook has 24K followers and TikTok 10.6K (owner screenshots 2026-06-12, correcting research/COMPETITORS.md). The FB audience is the most underused owned asset; IG should be grown by cross-posting the working TikTok formats, not built from scratch. (interviews/FOUNDER.md batch 2)
11. **No Padel365/Edenvale partnership exists** — never did. Edenvale's +60% plan must stand on local-area domination + delivery-threshold defence, not a partnership. (FOUNDER.md batch 2)
