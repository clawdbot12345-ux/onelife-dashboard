# One Life Health — Additional Lifestyle Image Prompts (Round 2)

## Global Brief

Generate the following lifestyle images for **One Life Health** (onelife.co.za). These complete the lifestyle imagery for all remaining pages.

**Quality standard:** Cinematic studio — think Thorne Research meets Tom Ford advertising. Shot on Hasselblad/Phase One, shallow depth of field, dramatic but warm lighting, magazine editorial quality. **No text, logos, or typography on ANY image.**

**Negative space:** Every image must leave 35–40% clean or dark negative space on either the left or right side for HTML text overlay (unless noted otherwise).

**Visual elements:** Premium supplement bottles (dark green with gold caps, amber glass, sleek white jars), South African botanicals (moringa, rooibos, buchu, protea), warm natural materials (slate, marble, walnut wood, linen).

**Colour palette:** Dark forest greens, warm golds, charcoal, cream, botanical greens.

**Output:** Photorealistic, no AI artifacts, no text/logos, consistent premium aesthetic.
