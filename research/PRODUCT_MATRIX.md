## Shape of the catalog (load-bearing facts)
- **Extreme long tail**: top 30 SKUs = ~26% of TTM online gross (R206k of R790k). No SKU exceeds 3% of revenue. There is **no hero product** — which is a strategy gap AND an opportunity (every international benchmark winner has one).
- **Vivid Health online = R17,669 TTM (54 orders, ~2.2% of sales)** despite a 50+ SKU range with sub-lines (IMMUNE / WOMAN / VIVID BODY / GUT HEALTH / STAY VIVID / VIVID NOURISHMENT). At 35–40% GP this is the most profitable shelf in the store and the least merchandised online.
- Category centre of gravity matches the ICP (women 35–50, high-LSM): #1 product is **Delfran Pcositol (PCOS)**, with 5-HTP, iron, thyroid, glycine, collagen all in the top 30.
