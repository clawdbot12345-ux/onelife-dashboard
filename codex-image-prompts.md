# One Life Health — Lifestyle Image Prompts for Codex

## Global Brief

Generate 12 lifestyle images for a premium South African health supplement e-commerce store called **One Life Health** (onelife.co.za).

**Quality standard:** Cinematic studio — think Thorne Research meets Tom Ford advertising. Shot on Hasselblad/Phase One, shallow depth of field, dramatic but warm lighting, magazine editorial quality. No text, logos, or typography on ANY image.

**Negative space:** Every image must leave 35–40% clean or dark negative space on either the left or right side for HTML text overlay.

**Visual elements across all images:** Premium supplement bottles (dark green with gold caps, amber glass, sleek white jars), South African botanicals (moringa, rooibos, buchu, protea), warm natural materials (slate, marble, walnut wood, linen).

**Colour palette:** Dark forest greens, warm golds, charcoal, cream, botanical greens.

**Output:** Photorealistic, no AI artifacts, no text or logos, consistent premium aesthetic across the full set.
