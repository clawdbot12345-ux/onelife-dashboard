community / reviews / events / Facebook-Shop *harvesting* channel — maintain it, don't
invest growth effort in it.

### Three brand-hygiene issues to fix immediately (free, high-impact)
1. **Handle inconsistency kills discoverability:** TikTok `@onelifehealthstore`, Instagram
   `@one_life_health_supermarket`, brand "Onelife Health", domain onelife.co.za. Unify to
   one handle everywhere (target `@onelifehealth` / `@onelifehealthsa`). A customer who
   finds you on one platform can't find you on the others right now.
2. **"Supermarket" undercuts premium positioning.** Your brand is a curated,
   practitioner-led apothecary (5,000+ products, 90+ brands, since 1996). "Supermarket" in
   the page names signals commodity. Rename to "Onelife Health".
3. **Bio = conversion, not decoration.** Every profile needs: what you are (SA's
   apothecary), the 3 store locations, one link (Linktree → shop / stores / WhatsApp /
   bestsellers), and a clear "DM us / chat to a consultant" CTA.

## Part 2 — Positioning & awareness strategy

You cannot out-shout Clicks (485K) or Dis-Chem (310K), and you don't need to. They are
**generic mass pharmacy**. Your share-of-voice play is to **own the premium,
practitioner-led, condition-specific niche on short-form video** — the lane the giants are
too broad to occupy and where misinformation is rampant (so credibility wins).

**Brand promise on social:** _"South Africa's apothecary — real people, real advice, the
supplements that actually work for your condition."_

That promise is also your moat against TikTok health misinformation: the research is clear
that **verified, credentialed, "explain-the-why" creators win.** You have qualified
consultants on the floor. Put them on camera — that is something no faceless e-commerce
competitor can copy.

## Part 3 — The content engine (5 pillars)

Built from your Omni data (Vitamins & Minerals is 58% of SKUs; live condition segments:
PCOS, GLP-1/metabolic, menopause, sleep/magnesium, joint, gut, immunity, stress, energy,
nootropics, adaptogens, autoimmune).

| # | Pillar | What it is | Why | Platforms |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Ask the Apothecary** | Presenter explains a condition + the 2–3 products that genuinely help | Your proven TikTok winner; credible; AEO-aligned | TikTok + Reels (lead) |
| 2 | **Ingredient deep-dives / trend-jacking** | "Should you take berberine/creatine/magnesium/ashwagandha?" — ride trending supplements *with professional nuance* | Trends bring reach; nuance is your differentiator vs misinformation | TikTok + Reels |
| 3 | **Behind-the-counter / human** | Store tours, "a day at Onelife", meet-the-consultant, new-arrival unboxings | Builds local trust + drives footfall to 3 stores | TikTok + Reels + Stories |
| 4 | **Social proof / UGC** | Customer stories, "what's flying off the shelf this week", reviews, transformations | UGC is 2.4× more trusted; cheap to produce | All |
| 5 | **Seasonal & events** | Tie to SA calendar + in-store events/workshops | Timeliness + footfall; converts followers to customers | All |

**Format rule (non-negotiable):** short-form vertical video is the growth engine on all
three platforms. **Stop posting static product images to *grow*** — use static
carousels/Stories only for depth and offers, never as the primary growth bet.

## Part 6 — Influencer / UGC (the awareness accelerator)

- Partner with **5–10 SA wellness micro-influencers (10K–50K)** — they out-ROI big
  creators (~82% of brands agree) and feel authentic. Source via SA wellness creator lists

- Run a light **UGC engine**: incentivise customers to post (loyalty points / monthly
  feature) → repost as Reels/Stories.

## Part 7 — Paid social (only after the organic engine runs)

Don't start with cold, agency-style paid creative. The cheapest, highest-ROI paid social
is **amplifying organic videos that already proved themselves:**
1. **Boost your top organic Reels/TikToks** (the ones with high saves/shares) to
   geo-targeted Centurion / Pretoria / Edenvale / Johannesburg audiences → awareness + footfall.
2. **Retarget** website + Klaviyo audiences with bestseller/condition video.
3. Scale only what shows positive ROAS. Let organic pick the winners; paid just adds reach.

## Part 8 — Targets & what to measure

**90-day targets**
- Instagram: **1.4K → 5K** followers (achievable purely by repurposing TikTok as Reels)
- TikTok: **10.6K → 16K**, with ≥1 video over 100K views
- Establish the weekly batch-shoot rhythm and unified handles/branding

**6-month targets**
- Instagram **10K+**, TikTok **20K+**, Facebook engagement up via Reels + events

**Metrics that actually matter (in order):**
1. **Saves + shares** (best purchase-intent signal on short-form)
2. Reach / non-follower reach (awareness growth)
3. Follower growth rate
4. Profile visits → link clicks → web sessions (Klaviyo/Shopify)
5. **In-store attribution:** add "How did you hear about us?" at the till + a TikTok/IG-only
   in-store code, so you can prove social → footfall (your R2M/month is in-store)

**Vanity metric to ignore:** raw post volume. You already have 1,286 IG posts; that number
has produced nothing. Quality video > volume.
