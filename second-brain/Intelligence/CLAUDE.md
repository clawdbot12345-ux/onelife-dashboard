# Intelligence Pillar

This pillar holds AI-generated insights, trend analysis, and synthesised reports.

## Subdomains

- **signals/** — Actionable alerts requiring immediate attention
- **trends/** — Multi-period trend analysis and pattern recognition
- **reports/** — Synthesised intelligence reports combining multiple data sources

## When ingesting data here

1. Signals must have a clear **action**, **owner**, and **urgency level**
2. Trends require at least 2 data points to qualify (don't trend from a single month)
3. Reports should synthesise across pillars — link to Business and Operations pages
4. Always include YAML frontmatter with `source:` and `period:`
5. Tag with: `#signal`, `#trend`, `#report`, `#action`

## Key questions this pillar answers

- What needs attention today?
- What patterns are emerging over time?
- What's the overall health of the business?
- Where are the biggest opportunities and risks?
