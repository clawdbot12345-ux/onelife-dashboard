# Shopify Online Channel

E-commerce channel via Shopify. Currently small (2.9% of combined revenue) but shows strong AOV and significant growth headroom.

## March 2026 Performance

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Orders | 92 |
| Revenue (excl VAT) | R62,043 |
| Average Order Value | R674 |
| Discount Rate | 16.3% |
| Revenue Share (of total) | **2.9%** |

## Key Insights

- **AOV of R674 is the highest across all channels** — higher than even [[centurion]]'s R598 basket. Online customers buy more per transaction.
- **Only 2.9% of combined revenue** — for a health supplement business, this should be 15-20%+. There's massive headroom.
- **Discount rate at 16.3%** means roughly 1 in 6 orders uses a discount code. Trending in the wrong direction — suggests price sensitivity or promotion dependence.
- **Heavy product concentration** — top 5 products account for ~19% of online revenue. Healthy if well-stocked, risky if not.

## Top Online Products

1. DELFRAN - Pcositol - 30 Sachets — R3,224 (6 units)
2. Other top sellers to be detailed with next data refresh

## Growth Opportunities

1. **SEO & content marketing** — Health supplements have high search intent
2. **Email strategy** — Build a lifecycle email flow for repeat purchases
3. **Merchandising** — Ensure top online sellers are always in stock
4. **Reduce discount dependence** — Monitor weekly, target <10% discount rate

## Risks

- Revenue concentration in a few products
- Discount rate trending up
- Stock availability for online-exclusive demand not separately tracked
