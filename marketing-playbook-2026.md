## 1. The market position (from SA gap map)
The SA market splits into: discount mass (Clicks/Dis-Chem/Takealot — fast or cheap, zero expertise), one ethical lifestyle leader (Faithful to Nature — CPT-bound, no advice, no BNPL, no subscriptions, ideologically excludes practitioner brands), and a fragmented premium long tail with 2010-era UX. **The #1 premium online health destination position is vacant** and One Life is the only player whose assets line up: apothecary heritage, real consultants, practitioner-brand relationships, 3 Gauteng stores, and a blog that already outranks billion-rand competitors on "best magnesium supplements South Africa".

**Positioning line:** *The anti-Dis-Chem. Not a warehouse — a person who knows your name and your stack.*

## 2. The five structural moves (sequenced)

### Q1 (now): Convert existing traffic — R120k → R170k
1. **Checkout stack (merchant, ~1 day):** enable Shop Pay + Apple Pay + Google Pay in Shopify Payments; install Payflex AND PayJustNow (WW has both, FTN has neither; BNPL lifts conversion 20–30% in SA). Add PayShap/Instant EFT via Ozow or PayFast if not present.
2. **PDP conversion kit (built this session on GROWTH theme):** free-delivery progress nudge, collect-free-in-store block, WhatsApp-consult guarantee, "pairs well with" basket builder targeting the 13k zero-converting commodity sessions.
3. **Klaviyo 6 flows (templates built this session):** welcome education series, abandoned checkout, browse abandonment, post-purchase how-to, replenishment reminder (30/60/90d by category), 60-day winback. Target: email = 15% of revenue within a quarter.
4. **Reviews engine:** Judge.me post-purchase review requests ON (via Klaviyo trigger), star schema in theme (built), review count beside PDP title.

### Q2: Recurring revenue + AOV — → R230k
5. **Subscribe & Save** (Appstle or Seal on Shopify): top 20 replenishables, 10–15% off + free delivery always, 30/45/60/90-day cadence. NOBODY major in SA offers this. 100 subscribers × R450 = R45k/month recurring floor.
6. **Dispensary Protocols** (Shopify native Bundles, free): 6–8 consultant-signed stacks — Sleep Ritual, Gut Reset, Winter Immunity, Stress & Adrenal, Joint Care, Energy. 10–15% bundle discount. AOV R594 → R680.
7. **Quiz → protocol funnel:** quiz output becomes a named protocol page with one-click add-all + segment-tagged Klaviyo follow-up.

### Q3–Q4: Audience + retention compounding — → R300k+
8. **Gauteng same-day moat:** "Order by 12pm, collect today / same-day courier in Gauteng." Market it everywhere. FTN physically cannot match this; Dis-Chem hides it in an app.
9. **Loyalty migration of foot traffic:** QR at all 3 tills — "R50 off your first online order + join Precious' list." Three stores of walk-ins = free audience no pure-play can touch.
10. **One Life Insider creator program:** 10–20 Gauteng nano-influencers (1–10k followers), product seeding + 10–15% commission codes, zero upfront (Arrae/iHerb model). Plus #MyOneLifeStack UGC draw.

## 3. The social engine (90-day plan, zero paid budget)
**Face:** Precious, One Life Health Consultant (already the byline on all 100 blog articles + Klaviyo emails — now give her a camera).
**Fix first:** consolidate handles (@onelifehealthstore everywhere; rename FB away from "Health Supermarket"; redirect the 22-follower Edenvale account). Link-in-bio hub → WhatsApp consult / shop / stores.

**The 5 repeatable formats (batch-film Mondays, 3hrs):**
1. **Ask Precious** — one real customer question, 45–90s talking head ("Magnesium glycinate vs citrate — which one do you need?")
2. **Shelf Tour / In-Store Find** — "5 things under R200 in our immunity aisle" (Dis-Chem proved hauls work in SA)
3. **Myth-Busting Monday** — IG carousel + cut-down Reel
4. **Consult Diaries** — anonymized consult stories ("A customer came in on 6 coffees a day…")
5. **Unbox & Taste-Test** + repost every customer haul that tags the store

**Cadence:** 4–5 TikToks, 4 Reels, 3 carousels, daily Stories, weekly WhatsApp broadcast.

**WhatsApp is the sleeper channel:** 94–96% of SA internet users; broadcast open rates ~98% vs ~20% email. Build "Precious' Wellness List": till QR + checkout opt-in (POPIA consent), weekly broadcast = Precious' Pick + 1 deal + catalog link. WhatsApp Business Catalog with top 30 SKUs, "reply ORDER" flow.

**KPIs:** Day 30: 100% calendar adherence, 50 WhatsApp subs. Day 60: 1,000 IG / 500 TikTok / 300 WhatsApp, first code-attributed orders. Day 90: 2,500 combined, 750 WhatsApp, 5% of orders code-attributed, 10+ active Insider creators.
