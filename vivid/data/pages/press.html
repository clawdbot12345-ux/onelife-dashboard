Brand summary (for journalist use)

Vivid Health is a South African plant-first supplement brand operated by Interlife Wholesalers (Pty) Ltd. Vivid is the formulation arm of the Onelife Health retail business, which has operated three Gauteng wellness stores in Centurion, Glen Village, and Edenvale since 1996. The brands differentiator is its dose-stated transparency: every active ingredient on every Vivid product is named with the exact milligrams, on the front of the label.

Founder bio (short)

Devakar Ramdass is the founder of Onelife Health and the formulator behind Vivid Health. After two decades selling third-party supplements through the familys retail stores, Devakar launched Vivid in 2020 to address what he describes as the supplement industrys "transparency problem": the standard practice of hiding active ingredient doses inside proprietary blends. Vivids 52 formulations are designed in collaboration with the in-house naturopath team and manufactured in a SAHPRA-aligned Centurion facility.
