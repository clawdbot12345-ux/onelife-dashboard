Log files

vividhealthsa.co.za follows the standard practice of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider, date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is not linked to anything personally identifiable, and is used for analysing trends, administering the site, tracking user movement, and gathering demographic information.

Cookies and web beacons

Like most websites, vividhealthsa.co.za uses cookies. These are small data files stored on your device. We use:

  • A session cookie to remember your cart between page loads.
  • Preference cookies to remember if you have dismissed the announcement bar or accepted cookie use.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Shopify Analytics) for anonymous usage tracking.
  • Marketing cookies (Klaviyo, Meta Pixel, where enabled) to measure email and ad performance — only if you opt in.

You can disable cookies in your browser. The site will still work, but your cart will not persist between visits and we will not be able to personalise your experience.

POPIA rights (your rights under South African law)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013, you have the right to:

  • Be informed — about what personal data we hold about you and why.
  • Access — request a copy of your personal data, free of charge, within a reasonable time.
  • Correction — fix anything that is incorrect or out of date.
  • Deletion — be forgotten, subject to legal retention requirements for tax and audit records.
  • Object — to direct marketing (one-click unsubscribe in every email) or to processing of your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Complain — to the Information Regulator if you are not satisfied with how we handled your request.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@vividhealthsa.co.za with proof of identity (a clear photo of your ID document alongside your face is sufficient). We respond within 7 business days.

CCPA privacy rights (California consumers)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to: request that we disclose what categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected about you; request deletion of personal data; and opt out of the sale of your personal data. We do not sell personal data. To make any of these requests, email privacy@vividhealthsa.co.za.

GDPR data protection rights (EU/UK visitors)

If you are based in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the same access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection rights as our POPIA rights above, exercised under the EU General Data Protection Regulation. Email privacy@vividhealthsa.co.za.

Children's information

Protecting the privacy of children is a special priority. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information on our site, please contact us immediately and we will use our best efforts to promptly remove it from our records.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Policy, or as required for legal, accounting, or audit obligations. Order and tax records are retained for 5 years as required by the South African Revenue Service.

International data transfers

Some of our service providers (Shopify, Klaviyo, Google) are based outside South Africa and may process your data in jurisdictions with different privacy laws. We rely on Shopify's, Klaviyo's, and Google's contractual data protection commitments and standard contractual clauses to ensure your information is treated with the same care as it would be under POPIA.
