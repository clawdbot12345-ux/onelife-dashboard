## 1. Market Context

### TAM & competitive landscape
- **SA VMS market**: ~R12–14bn retail (Euromonitor 2024); online VMS estimated **R900m–R1.2bn** and growing ~18% YoY (Statista Digital Market Outlook SA, 2024).
- **E-commerce penetration**: 38% of South Africans bought online in past month (We Are Social/Meltwater "Digital 2024 SA"); Gauteng + WC = ~70% of online spend.
- **Key online competitors (rough monthly online revenue, SimilarWeb traffic × 1.5% conv × AOV)**:
  - Faithful to Nature — ~R8–12m/mo (broad natural)
  - Wellness Warehouse — ~R6–9m/mo online
  - Dis-Chem online — VMS slice ~R15–20m/mo
  - Coyne Healthcare — ~R2–3m/mo D2C
  - Lifematrix / Nutritech / SSN — sports-leaning, R3–5m/mo each
  - Takealot's VMS category — the elephant; ~R25–35m/mo
- **Gap Vivid can own**: plant-first + exact-mg transparency + clinician-grade dosing. Closest analogues globally: AG1, Heights, WeAreFeel. SA has no clear winner in this slot.

## 2. Paid Acquisition

### Meta (FB + IG) — primary channel
**Budget bands to test:**
- Weeks 1–4: R400/day (R12k/mo) — learning
- Weeks 5–12: R1,000/day (R30k/mo)
- Months 4–6: R1,800–2,500/day (R55–75k/mo) at proven ROAS

**Account structure (CBO + Advantage+ hybrid):**
- **Top of funnel (60%)**: Advantage+ Shopping; broad targeting, 25–55, SA, exclude purchasers 180d
- **Interest stacks (20%)**: Yoga, Wellness, Banting, Discovery Vitality, CrossFit ZA, plant-based
- **Lookalikes (10%)**: 1–3% LAL of purchasers + 90-day ATC + quiz completers
- **Retargeting (10%)**: 7/14/30-day visitors, quiz abandoners, ATC; DPAs

**Hook formulas that work for SA supplements:**
1. *"I stopped taking 7 supplements and replaced them with one. Here's what happened in 30 days."*
2. *"South African pharmacists keep recommending this — but Dis-Chem doesn't stock it."*
3. *"The label says 400mg magnesium. Most brands give you 40mg of actual elemental. Here's the difference."* (transparency angle — Vivid's moat)
4. *"Highveld hay-fever hitting? This quercetin + bromelain stack changed my September."* (seasonal)
5. *"R23/day for the multi a Sandton functional doctor charges R1,200 for."*

**Creative formats (test 3 per week):** UGC talking head 9:16, founder-led "why we started Vivid" 60s, static carousels mg-for-mg vs competitor, "GRWM + supplements" reel, quiz-led ad.

**SA benchmarks:** CPM R80–280, CTR 1.2–2.2%, LP conv 1.8–3.5%, CPA cold R250–450, CPA RT R90–180, ROAS month 3+ 3.0–4.5x.

## 6. Retention & LTV

- **Subscription**: lead with *"Skip, swap or cancel anytime"*; reposition 10% as **"Vivid Members save R[X]/year"**. Member perks: free shipping at R300, early access, birthday gift. Target sub = 25% of revenue by mo 6.
- **Referral**: Give R75 / Get R75 via ReferralCandy or Friendbuy. Push in PP email #5 + WhatsApp.
- **Loyalty (Smile.io or LoyaltyLion)**: 1 pt/R1, 100 pts = R10. Bonus pts: review (50), quiz (25), birthday (200).
- **Reviews**: stay on **Judge.me** (best Shopify ROI for SA SMBs); auto-request day 21; photo review = R50 voucher; sync to Google Shopping seller + product reviews via schema. Skip Yotpo until R1m/mo.
- **Reactivation**: 60/90/120-day flows + Meta RT custom audience of "past purchaser, no order 60d"; bring back with bundle discount, not single-product discount.

## 9. KPIs

### Weekly
- Revenue, orders, AOV, conv rate
- Blended ROAS, per-channel ROAS, per-channel CPA
- Quiz completion → purchase rate
- Email: revenue, list growth, flow rev split
- Organic: follower growth %, top 3 posts, avg reach
- Subs started / cancelled

### Monthly
- LTV (90d/180d), repeat rate, sub % of revenue
- Cohort retention curve
- Channel mix vs target
- Influencer ROI (codes + UTMs)
- Review volume + avg rating
- LTV:CAC (target 3:1 by mo 6)

### Quarterly
- TAM share estimate, brand search volume (Google Trends "vivid health")
- NPS (post-purchase survey)
- Subscriber churn
- Persona discovery via quiz data
- ROAS sustainability at higher spend
